Music

Hailing from Bewdley, United Kingdom, Will Holland has become a heavy hitter in the latin inspired fusion music scene. Originating under the pseudonym 'Quantic' with the release of his first album The 5th Exotic in 2001 - Holland's musical form has undergone dramatic change; covering a eclectic range of genres from trip-hop, funk, soul, jazz and finally through to latin inspired electronica with his latest 2019 release 'Atlantic Oscillations'.

With eighteen albums (and counting) under his belt, Holland has been a prolific contributor to the music scene and has been dubbed a " One-Man Music Factory" by Rolling Stone and there's no sign he is stopping anytime soon...

Expect: hip-hop style beats, latin influenced grooves, percussion and melodies, electronic overtones and unforgettable earworms that will be with you for days after...
